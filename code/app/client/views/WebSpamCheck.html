Web Spam Check

  • - Most of the spam websites consist of word count between 750 and 1500 words
  • - Spam pages tend to use far more KeyWords(words other than "and","or","I","it",etc) in the TITLE compared to non-spam pages.
  • - The Website's Top Level Domain will be checked whether it's part of the most abused/misused list of domains
  • - Websites with a high percentage of anchor text (to standard text) are more likely to be spam sites.
